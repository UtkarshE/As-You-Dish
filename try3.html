Samosa

A samosa is a fried or baked pastry with a savory filling, including ingredients such as spiced potatoes, onions and peas. Generally it is triangular or cone shaped and accompanied by chutney. Samosas are a popular entrée, appetizer, or snack

Butter Chicken

Butter chicken is a type of curry made from chicken with a spiced tomato and butter sauce. Its sauce is known for its rich texture. In addition sometimes the cream gives the curry sauce a silky smooth rich texture. It is popularly served with naan bread or rice.

Gulab Jamun

Gulab jamun is a milk-solid-based sweet, originating in the Indian subcontinent. It is made mainly from milk solids, traditionally from khoya, which is milk reduced to the consistency of a soft dough. It is often garnished with dried nuts such as almonds and cashews to enhance flavour.
